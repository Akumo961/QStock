## Getting Started

### What is QR Inventory System?

QR Inventory System is a modern inventory management application designed for Scouts Musulmans de Montréal. It allows you to:

- 📱 **Scan QR codes** to quickly borrow and return items
- 📦 **Browse inventory** to see available equipment
- 👤 **Manage your profile** and track your borrowing history
- 🔔 **Receive notifications** for due dates and overdue items
- 📊 **View statistics** about your borrowing activity

### System Requirements

- **Web Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
- **Mobile:** iOS 12+ or Android 8+
- **Camera:** Required for QR code scanning
- **Internet:** Required for most features

## Logging In

### First Time Login

1. Open the QR Inventory System website
2. Click **"Login"** button
3. Enter your credentials:
   - **Email:** Your organization email
   - **Password:** Provided by administrator
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### Forgot Password?

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click link and create new password
5. Login with new password

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Profile** page
2. Click **"Change Password"** button
3. Enter:
   - Current password
   - New password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm new password
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

## Scanning QR Codes

### How to Scan

1. Click **"Scan QR Code"** on dashboard
2. Allow camera access when prompted
3. Point camera at QR code
4. System automatically detects and processes code

### Types of QR Codes

**User QR Code (Blue border)**
- Your personal QR code
- Found on your profile
- Identifies you when borrowing

**Item QR Code (Green border)**
- Attached to each item
- Contains item information
- Used for borrowing and returning

### Scanning Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Hold phone steady
- Ensure good lighting
- Keep QR code flat and visible
- Clean camera lens if needed

❌ **DON'T:**
- Move too quickly
- Block the QR code
- Use in very dim lighting
- Use damaged/wrinkled codes

## Borrowing Items

### Method 1: Scan QR Codes (Recommended)

1. Click **"Scan QR Code"**
2. **First:** Scan your user QR code
3. **Second:** Scan the item QR code
4. Enter details:
   - **Quantity:** How many (if multiple available)
   - **Purpose:** Why you need it (optional)
   - **Duration:** How long you need it
5. Click **"Confirm Borrow"**
6. ✅ Success! Item is now borrowed

### Method 2: Browse and Borrow

1. Go to **"Inventory"** page
2. Find item you want:
   - Use search bar
   - Filter by category
   - Browse list
3. Click **"Borrow"** button on item card
4. Fill in borrowing details
5. Confirm borrowing

### Borrowing Rules

- ⚠️ Maximum **5 active borrows** at once
- ⚠️ Must return overdue items before new borrows
- ⚠️ Default borrow period: **7 days** (varies by item)
- ⚠️ Can request extension before due date

### What Happens After Borrowing?

- Item marked as "Borrowed" in system
- Due date calculated and stored
- You receive confirmation email
- Reminder sent 2 days before due date
- Overdue notification if not returned on time

## Returning Items

### Method 1: Scan QR Code

1. Click **"Scan QR Code"**
2. Switch to **"Return"** tab
3. Scan the item QR code
4. Select condition:
   - ✅ **Excellent:** Perfect condition
   - ✅ **Good:** Minor wear
   - ⚠️ **Fair:** Some damage
   - ❌ **Poor:** Significant damage
5. Add notes if needed
6. Click **"Confirm Return"**

### Method 2: From My Borrows

1. Go to **"My Profile"** > **"Active Borrows"** tab
2. Find the item to return
3. Click **"Return"** button
4. Select condition and add notes
5. Confirm return

### Return Checklist

Before returning, ensure:
- ✅ Item is clean
- ✅ All parts included
- ✅ No visible damage
- ✅ Works properly
- ✅ Returned to correct location

### Late Returns

If item is returned late:
- ⚠️ Late return recorded in your history
- ⚠️ May affect future borrowing privileges
- ⚠️ Late fees may apply (if configured)

## FAQ

### Can I borrow items for someone else?
No, you can only borrow items for yourself. The person who borrows must return the item.

### What if I need an item longer?
Contact an administrator before the due date to request an extension.

### What if an item is damaged when I borrow it?
Report it immediately before borrowing. Take photos if possible.

### Can I reserve items in advance?
Currently, reservations are not available. Items are first-come, first-served.

### What happens if I lose an item?
Report it immediately to an administrator. Replacement costs may apply.

### How do I get a new QR code?
Contact an administrator if your QR code is lost or damaged.

### Can I see who else has borrowed an item?
No, borrowing history is private for security reasons.

### What if the camera won't scan?
- Clean your camera lens
- Ensure good lighting
- Try manual entry instead
- Contact admin if issues persist

### Do I need the app installed?
No, you can use the web browser. But installing makes it easier to access.

### Can I use multiple devices?
Yes, login from any device with your credentials.
